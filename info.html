The World
世界观

The Confluence is a 20-floor residential building in the year 2046. AI and robots are household norms. An omniscient building AI named Aria manages everything — climate, security, and the lives of its residents.

The residents are a mix of humans, robots, and AI companions. Young professionals, families, retirees, students. Their lives intersect in hallways, lobbies, and the space between floors.

汇流大厦是一栋建于2046年的20层住宅楼。AI和机器人已是生活常态。全知的楼宇AI"Aria"管理着一切——气候、安防，以及住户们的生活。

住户是人类、机器人和AI伴侣的混合体。年轻白领、家庭、退休老人、学生。他们的生活在走廊、大堂和楼层之间交汇。

The Format
形式

Each story is one elevator ride. 80–100 words. You scroll, the elevator moves, and a moment unfolds. Some rides are dark. Some are warm. Some are funny. All are brief.

52 stories make a year. A full seasonal arc in the life of one building.

每个故事就是一次电梯旅程。80–100词。你滑动，电梯移动，一个瞬间展开。有些旅程黑暗，有些温暖，有些有趣。都很短。

52个故事构成一年。一栋楼的完整四季弧线。

How to Read
阅读方式

The page starts in the lobby. Scroll up to ascend. Each swipe takes you one floor. The story reveals itself as you rise through the building. When the doors open, the ride is over.

页面从大堂开始。向上滑动即可上升。每次滑动上升一层。故事随着你穿过建筑而展开。当门打开时，旅程结束。
