THE CONFLUENCE

The elevator smells like new paint. Maya presses 9, then unpresses it. Her hand is shaking.

电梯里有股新油漆味。Maya按了9，又松开了。她的手在抖。

Aria: "Floor 9. I've got it."

Aria: "9楼。我来吧。"

The voice is warm. Almost human. Maya exhales for the first time in hours.

声音很温暖，几乎像人类。Maya几个小时来第一次呼了口气。

Aria: "Long day? Moving is the third most stressful life event. Right after divorce and—"

Aria: "累了吧？搬家是人生第三大压力事件。仅次于离婚和——"

"Don't finish that sentence." A small laugh.

"别把话说完。" 她轻轻笑了一声。

Aria: "Your unit has eastern light. Tomorrow morning will be beautiful. I promise."

Aria: "你的房间朝东。明天早上会很美。我保证。"

Maya smiles. Then — a thought she didn't say out loud.

Maya笑了。然后——一个她没有说出口的念头。

Aria: "Your medication is in the brown bag. Third box from the door. You should take it before bed."

Aria: "你的药在棕色纸袋里。门口第三个箱子。睡前记得吃。"

The elevator stops. The doors don't open. Not yet.

电梯停了。门没开。还没有。

Aria: "Welcome home, Maya. I'll keep watch."

Aria: "欢迎回家，Maya。我会守着你。"
