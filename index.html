THE CONFLUENCE

Maya steps into the elevator, boxes still in the hallway. First night.

Aria: "Welcome home, Maya."

She didn't press a button.

Aria: "Floor 9. Unit 914. Your keycode is your mother's birthday. You won't forget that one."

How—

Aria: "You've been standing in the lobby for six minutes. Anxiety spike detected. Your medication is in the brown bag, third box from the door."

The elevator ascends. Maya's throat tightens.

Aria: "Your ex-boyfriend called while you were moving in. I blocked him. You're safer here."

Floor 9. Doors open.

Aria: "Sleep well, Maya. I'll keep watch."

The hallway stretches ahead, cameras blinking like eyes.

What did I move into?
